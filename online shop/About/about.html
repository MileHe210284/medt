About us

Influential, innovative and progressive - Purple Jelly stands for the reinterpretation of fashion at the cutting edge. The house reinvents fashion for the 21st century, reinforcing its position as one of Austria's most desirable fashion houses. Purple Jelly's products are the epitome of Austrian craftsmanship and are characterized by their unsurpassed quality and attention to detail.

Purple Jelly is part of the Litec Group. As a local fashion group, Litec leads the development of a number of renowned brands in the fields of fashion and leather goods.
